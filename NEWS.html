Jorge Masvidal: Lookin' for a Fight

Fan-favorite welterweight contender Jorge Masvidal said that he is looking for any welterweight contender above him that will give him a third shot at the 170 lb title even after his brutal knockout loss to Champion Kamaru Usman at UFC 261. #1 contender and former friend/training partner of Jorge, Colby Covington, is scheduled to rematch Usman at UFC 268 on November 6th at Madison Square Garden. #2 Gilbert Burns has accepted a fight with Masvidal, but nothing official has been done. #3 Leon Edwards, who has a storied past with Jorge, is looking forward to a shot at Kamaru Usman. Finally, #5 Vicente Luque has had developing stories centered around a potential scrap with fan favorite Nate Diaz.
